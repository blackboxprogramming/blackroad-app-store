## Executive Summary

**BlackRoad OS** is an alternative app store platform that eliminates the traditional 30% commission fee charged by Apple and Google, allowing developers to keep 100% of their revenue.

### The Opportunity
- Global app market: $935 billion (2023)
- Apple/Google duopoly charging 15-30% commission
- 5+ million developers paying $99-125/year + commission
- Growing frustration with gatekeepers

### The Solution
A PWA-first app store that:
- Charges 0% commission
- Has $0 base fees
- Publishes apps instantly
- Never rejects apps
- Is open source

## Risk Analysis

### Risks
1. **Platform risk** - Apple/Google could block PWAs
   - *Mitigation:* Also support native apps
   
2. **Low adoption** - Developers don't switch
   - *Mitigation:* Make migration dead simple
   
3. **Competition** - Others build similar platforms
   - *Mitigation:* Open source creates moat
   
4. **Legal** - App stores sue for competition
   - *Mitigation:* 100% legal, PWAs are just websites

### Opportunities
1. EU Digital Markets Act forces Apple to allow alternatives
2. Growing developer frustration with 30% tax
3. Rise of PWA technology
4. Open source community support
